This is a shiny app that allows us to visualiza some saving/investing scenarios:

mode1: Invest amount of money in the begining at a annual rate of return 

mode2: Invest amount of money with a fixed annuity at a annual rate of return 

mode3: Invest amount of money with growing annuity at a annual rate of return

This shiny app can simulate and compare different scenarios by selecting Initial Amount, Annual Contribution, Return Rate, Growth Rate, Years and Whether to facet or not.
